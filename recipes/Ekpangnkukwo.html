Ekpangnkukwo

This is delicious and yummy Ekpangnkukwo

Ingredients

  • Water yam
  • Coco yam
  • Coco yam leaves
  • Bonka fish
  • Periwinkle
  • Palm oil
  • Cray fish

Steps

  1. Peel and crate the Water yam
  2. Peel and crate the cocoyam
  3. Mix together and roll
  4. Parboil and add other ingredients to taste
